Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 24, 2023 | Version 4.2.0-Noir

StepX Noir is committed to the absolute transparency of your digital footprint. This document outlines the protocols we employ to secure your data within the Kinetic Vault, ensuring your privacy is as guarded as the artifacts we curate.

1.0

Data Collection

We collect information that facilitates the high-velocity curation experience. This includes biometric dimensions for fit optimization, transaction signatures, and encrypted communication logs.

  • Personal Identifiers: Legal name, encrypted shipping destination, and cryptographic wallet addresses.
  • Technical Artifacts: IP addresses, device architecture, and interaction telemetry within the Vault.
2.0

Security Protocols

"Security is not a feature; it is the foundation of the Vault."

All data is subjected to AES-256 bit encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 during transit. Our internal access is governed by zero-trust architecture, ensuring that your personal curation remains inaccessible to unauthorized entities.

3.0

Third-Party Integrity

We do not sell, lease, or distribute your identity to mass-market advertisers. Shared data is limited strictly to logistics partners (e.g., global couriers) and secure payment gateways necessary to finalize a transaction within the StepX ecosystem.

4.0

Your Right to Erasure

As a member of StepX Noir, you maintain the absolute right to have your digital footprint purged from our primary servers. Upon request, we will initiate the "Cold Exit" protocol, removing all non-regulatory personal data within 72 hours.
